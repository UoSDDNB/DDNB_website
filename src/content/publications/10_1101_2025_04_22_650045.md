abstract: "Abstract                                    Single-cell technologies now span multiple modalities, generating large, complex datasets that challenge analysis and sharing. We present                   ShinyCell2                   , an enhanced R package for interactive visualisation of single-cell multi-omics and spatial transcriptomics data.                   ShinyCell2                   retains the simplicity and lightweight deployment of its predecessor while introducing advanced visualisations, cross-modality comparisons, and statistical tools tailored to spatial and multi-omic data. It enables intuitive, rapid exploration of high-dimensional data without requiring extensive computational expertise."
